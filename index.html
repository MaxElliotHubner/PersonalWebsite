The Institute of Mathematics at the University of Oxford awards a small number of D.Phil. scholarships. Candidates are put forward by the individual research groups of the department and selected on the merit of their research.

Promotionsstipendium der Studienstiftung des Deutschen Volkes

In addition to its undergraduate scholarship the Studienstiftung des Deutschen Volkes (German Academic Scholarship Foundation) runs a separate and more competitive competition for doctoral scholarships. These are awarded for a maximum of 3 years and grants a living cost stipend.

Oct. 2017 - Sept. 2020

DAAD Scholarship for Studies Abroad

Among other endeavours the DAAD (German Academic Exchange Service) supports German undergraduates and graduate students in seeking international academic experience. Also founded in 1925 it is the largest German support organisation in the field of international academic co-operation. On the basis of academic merit it awards a stipend at 800€/month, supports travel costs and covers tuition fees up to 6.100€.

Oct. 2016 - Sept. 2017

Stipendium der Studienstiftung for Studies Abroad

The Studienstiftung des Deutschen Volkes (German Academic Scholarship Foundation) invites for applications to generously support 1-2 year undergraduate and master studies abroad. It awards a stipend of 350€/month, supports travel costs and covers up to 10.000€ of annual tuition fees.

Oct. 2016 - Sept. 2017

Max-Weber Scholarship

Max Weber was a German sociologist, jurist, and political economist and an influential theorists on the development of modern Western society. The Max-Weber Scholarship support German students studying at Bavarian universities. The programm emphasizes the personal development of students. In conjunction with academic support it awards a stipend of 215€/month.

April 2015 - Sept. 2017

Scholarship of the Studienstiftung des Deutschen Volkes

The Studienstiftung des Deutschen Volkes (German Academic Scholarship Foundation) is Germany's largest and oldest scholarship foundation. It was founded in 1925 with the goal to support promising individuals. The Studienstiftung is non-political, non-denominational and ideologically independent. The scholarship is awarded to less than 0.5% of all students annually and includes a stipend of 300€/month and academic support.

April 2015 - Sept. 2017

Deutschlandstipendium

Introduced in 2011 the Deutschlandstipendium is a scholarship awarded to university students at German universities for academic excellence. An average of 1.5% of all students are awarded this scholarship annually. It awards a stipend of 300€/month and offers academic support.

Oct. 2014 - Oct. 2015
